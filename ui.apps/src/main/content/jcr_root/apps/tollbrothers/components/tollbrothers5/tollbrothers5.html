Continuing a treasured tradition of music.

For nearly 20 years, Toll Brothers has been the main sponsor of the Metropolitan Opera Saturday Matinee Radio Broadcasts, the longest-running continuous classical music program in American radio history. The program began in 1931 and, since then, Metropolitan Opera has aired over 1,800 live broadcasts of nearly 200 different operas.

Today, the Met radio broadcasts are aired over the Toll Brothers-Metropolitan Opera International Radio Network, which comprises over 300 stations in the United States and reaches 11 million people in 42 countries around the globe.

Touching Lives Across the World

Toys for Tots

Toll Brothers organizes annual collections in support of the Marine Toys for Tots program. In our divisions throughout the country, including Florida, New York, California, Nevada, and our Pennsylvania corporate headquarters, we purchase gift cards and new toys for families in need. Toll Brothers is honored to bring joy to many children (and their parents) each holiday season.

Operation Holidays from Home

Seeds of Peace

Operation Holidays from Home is a Toll Brothers initiative that benefits members of the military. Employees donate snacks, magazines, toiletries, and other creature comforts, then send them in care packages to units deployed away from home during the holiday season.

Each summer, the Seeds of Peace Camp program in Otisfield, Maine, offers a diverse group of young people from the United States, the Middle East, and South Asia the opportunity to form relationships, develop their leadership abilities, and build valuable communication skills as they address complex topics. Thus far, alumni number over 8,000 individuals representing 27 countries.

Fund the Cure Sponsorship

The Jane and Bob Toll Fund at Seeds of Peace provides vital support through scholarships and operational investments, and hundreds of Toll Brothers employees, associates, friends, and family devote a full day to preparing the Seeds of Peace campground for the summer season each year.
